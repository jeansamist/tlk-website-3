Common Questions

Answers within an hour
01
How do I know what my home is really worth?

We provide personalized market evaluations based on real local data — not just automated online estimates. Terri walks every block, knows the comps that actually closed, and accounts for property-specific factors algorithms miss: condition, upgrades, school zones, and current local buyer demand.

02
Should I renovate before selling?

Not always. We help you understand which improvements may increase value and which expenses may not be necessary. Sometimes a $300 paint refresh moves the needle further than a $30,000 kitchen remodel — we'll tell you straight.

03
How long will it take to sell?

Every property is different, but homes we list in Muskegon Heights typically move within 2–4 weeks when priced correctly. We share realistic timelines up front based on current inventory and absorption rates — no false promises.

04
Will I be locked into a high-pressure process?

No. We believe in honest guidance, clear communication, and helping you make informed decisions at your own pace. If now isn't the right time, we'll tell you — and we'll still be here when you're ready.

05
What fees should I expect?

Complete transparency from day one. You'll see a full breakdown of commission, closing costs, and any optional services before you sign anything. No surprises at the table, and no fees we haven't already discussed.
